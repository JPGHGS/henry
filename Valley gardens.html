Valley Gardens

Valley Gardens is a 17-acre English Heritage Grade II listed garden in regal Low Harrogate, next to the Pinewoods woodlands.

Facilities include: Skate park suitable for BMX, skateboards and scooters, band concerts on Sunday afternoons from June to the end of Augustm, paddling pool (seasonal), games pavilion selling light refreshments (winter opening, weekends only 11am-3pm), games in park including tennis, pitch and putt and crazy golf, Magnesia Well cafe selling light refreshments - winter opening hours 8.30am-4pm (subject to change), toilets, children's boating pool, childrens playground and orienteering.

This attraction could originally appear boring for teens but there are many things bored teenagers can get up to. This could include skating or learning to skate at the skatepark or playing tennis or even just chilling out on the grass with friends if its a sunny day.
