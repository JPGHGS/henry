Climbing Wall Centre

Harrogate Climbing Centre is an indoor oasis for climbers of all ages and abilities. The walls are awash with 65 climbing lines of all shapes and sizes, some soaring 15 metres tall, while bouldering can be practised in two dedicated areas. A cafe, gear shop and meeting room round out the facilities, while a variety of courses, coaching, kids' clubs and birthday party services offer something for everyone.

Some teens dismiss indoor climbing as being a fun thing to do but climbing has endless opportunities on different levels of climbing you can do such as bouldering, autobelay and more.
