Rossett Sports Centre

Rossett Sports Centre is a non-membership dry site sports centre, offering first class sports facilities to the local community. The centre is open 7 days a week and its wide range of facilities can be casually booked or block booked throughout the year.

Such facilities available for hire show as follow- 3G pitch, sports hall, squash courts, tennis and netball courts, changing facilities and a gymnasium.

Many teens in Harrogate may usually play football and other sports on a local green or in their garden and even on their roads. Rossett Sports Centre provides all the space and equiptment teens need to play the sports and acitivities they want.
